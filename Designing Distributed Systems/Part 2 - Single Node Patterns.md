- In general, the goal of a container is to establish boundaries around specific resources. The boundary delineates team ownership and provides separation of concerns
- Reasons to split an application into multiple containers:
	- Resource isolation
	- separation of concerns

- _ambassador pattern_ - brokers interactions between the application container to add functionality
- The value of the _ambassador pattern_ is twofold:
	- inherent value in building modular, reusable containers
	- separation of concerns
